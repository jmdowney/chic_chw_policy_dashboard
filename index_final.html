HOW TO USE THE DASHBOARD.

This dashboard shows proCHW policies across Countdown to 2030 countries. Explore the map to see where policies exist, what they include, and whether there is evidence that they are being implemented. Scroll past the map to view methods and definitions.

Getting Started

Use the buttons at the top of the map to switch between indicators, including accredited CHWs, paid CHWs, accredited and paid CHWs, policy status, and evidence of CHW policy implementation.

Use the download button in the bottom right corner to download a high-resolution image of the map or the raw data.

Explore the Map

Click on a country to view policy details in the sidebar. You can also use the search bar to quickly find a specific country.

The legend at the bottom left explains the color coding for each indicator.

View Country Details

The sidebar provides detailed information for each country, including:

  • CHW groups
  • Policy details
  • Commitments related to CHW accreditation, pay, supervision, training, and supplies
  • Evidence that policies are being implemented
  • A timeline of key policy milestones
  • AI-generated country summaries

Click the expandable sections to view this information.

Contribute Data or Corrections

We want to hear from you! Please use the link in the sidebar to submit information or corrections. Community contributions help keep the dashboard accurate and up to date.

WHAT WOULD YOU LIKE TO DOWNLOAD?

Note the map image option downloads the current map view. If you wish to download a different view, please go back to the map, select your desired indicator, and zoom to your desired map area before coming back to download your image.

SUBMIT FOR REVIEW.

Updates are reviewed on a rolling basis. Our team will review and respond to submissions within 6 weeks. Approved changes are reflected in quarterly updates.
